Welcome to BLACK'S

PAPER STORE & GIFT SHOP

Since the year 1867, we've been dishing out exquisite gifts.

C'mon in

|| Back yonder in 1860

The establishment kicked off its days as The Paper Store, situated right within what's now known as the Mountain Tops Building. But the winds of change blew through in '57, when a fella named George Black hitched his wagon to the place, and lo and behold, he decided to christen it anew as BLACK'S Paper Store and Gift Shop.

|| Time marched on

As it's wont to do, and by the time them 1970's rolled around, a couple by the names of Dick and Ruth Pheeney stepped into the picture, got themselves the store. Then, come 1983, the establishment changed hands yet again, falling into the palms of a certain David C. Hemenway.

|| Fast-forwarding through the years

Like a river's current, we find ourselves in 2007, a year when Chris and Cindy Patten rode into town and staked their claim on the store, becoming the present-day proprietors. And that, is the twisty tale of how this here store journeyed through time, passin' from one set of hands to another like a prized pocket watch.
